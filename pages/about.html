Our Story

It all began one day when Kason went to his pantry to get some gluten-free pasta (a staple in his house) for his dinner. He realized that he had run out and he couldn't believe it! He ALWAYS had gluten-free pasta in his pantry, but somehow, he forgot to buy some the last time he went to the store.

He was telling his friends Ryan, Stefanie, and Rahzae about his frustration, and they all chimed in with their own similar stories, and how they wished there was a way to track their grocery items so they could replace them before they ran out, or ensure they were used before they passed their expiration date.

And foodWise was born....
